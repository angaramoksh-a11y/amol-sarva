Investing

Backing founders

The fund

Life Extension Ventures

Co-founded in 2022, Life Extension Ventures is a venture firm for people and planet: longevity and health on one side, climate on the other, and — running through both — founders using AI to accelerate the pace of science.

It is where most of Amol’s new investing now happens, carrying the same instinct that ran through the angel years: back the people building the thing, early, and help them ship.
